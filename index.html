LOCATION

Establiments is a small, ancient village nestled in the foothills of the Tramuntana, just ten minutes from Palma. Surrounded by olive groves, pine forests, and dry-stone walls, it offers the quietude of rural Mallorca without sacrificing proximity to the city. The valley has been a retreat for artists, architects, and those who seek a deliberate pace.

De House sits within this landscape — not announcing itself, but belonging to it. The property commands uninterrupted views across the valley to the sea, framed by mature gardens and a generous plot that ensures absolute privacy.
